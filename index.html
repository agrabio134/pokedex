Welcome to Pokedex Tracker

Pokedex Tracker is a cutting-edge platform built on the Sui blockchain, designed to help Pokémon enthusiasts track and catalog their NFT collections with a retro, pixelated aesthetic inspired by classic Pokémon games.

Whether you're a casual collector or a competitive trainer, our system provides a seamless experience to manage your Pokémon NFTs, view detailed stats, and share your progress with the community.

Why Choose Pokedex Tracker?

Blockchain Security

Leverage the power of Sui blockchain for secure and transparent tracking of your Pokémon NFT collection.

Retro Design

Enjoy a pixelated, Game Boy-inspired interface that brings nostalgia to every interaction.

Community Driven

Connect with other trainers, share your Pokedex, and compete on leaderboards.

Tokenomics
